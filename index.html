NATURAL RESOUCE DEPLETION IS A REAL THING.

We Filipinos use up natural resources in everyday life even if we do not know we are doing so. The jeepney we ride to school or work relies on fossil fuels from deep inside the earth to function. The electronic devices we used were made using minerals that are mined from the earth. The 100-peso bill we use to buy siomai from the cafeteria came from plants that are chosen by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas for currency production. Even the siomai we bought came from natural resources as well; specifically, living animals. Even though natural resources seem to be infinite due to their many uses, they are gradually decreasing in amount more and more intensively due to our consumption, and, at some point, all of them could be gone. This is natural resource depletion, and it is a concept that all of us Filipinos should take note of to save Mother Earth.
