Pearls of the Orient is a website created to spread awareness about the depletion of natural resources in the Philippines. It aims to encourage people to take action and apply helpful practices in real life through making individual efforts, supporting advocacies, and fighting for wide-scale change.

The Creators

Grandis Frias

Grandis, 15, is a student at the Philippine Science High School Main Campus. She is a youth advocate for accessible education and has applied knowledge from this advocacy to co-create Pearls of the Orient. She brings this campaign to society through other extra-curricular projects.

Jini Dela Paz

Jini is a Grade 9 student also studying at PSHS-Main Campus. Some of his hobbies are reading books, coding, and research, all of which he utilized to make this website. He thinks that natural source depletion is a very important subject since people do not realize its potential dangers to society. He also hopes that the website will fulfill its goal of educating and spreading awareness about the topic.
